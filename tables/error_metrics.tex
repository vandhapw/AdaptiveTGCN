MAE & $\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}|y_i - \hat{y}_i|$ \\[0.3em]
SMAPE & $\frac{100}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}\frac{2|y_i - \hat{y}_i|}{|y_i| + |\hat{y}_i|}$ \\[0.3em]
R² Score & $1 - \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i - \hat{y}_i)^2}{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(y_i - \bar{y})^2}$ \\
